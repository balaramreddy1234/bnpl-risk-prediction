🔍 Decision Transparency

Our BNPL Strategic Engine uses an advanced Random Forest Machine Learning model to ensure that every credit decision is proactive, fair, and transparent.

How the Model Decides

The model analyzes seven key financial and behavioral variables to calculate a risk probability score. Decisions are based on data, not bias, ensuring a fair evaluation for every user.

📊 Debt-to-Income Ratio

We analyze if your EMI obligations exceed 50% of your income. High ratios suggest a higher risk of default.

⏱️ Repayment Behavior

Consistent on-time payments lower your risk. Multiple critical delays (5+) increase the probability of default.

💳 Credit Capacity

The model checks if the requested loan exceeds your calculated capacity (Income × Tenure).

📈 CIBIL Recovery

For low credit scores, we prioritize users who show reliable behavioral patterns despite their past history.

Proactive Intervention

If the model identifies you as "High Risk," it doesn't just stop there. Our engine provides Smart Suggestions—like adjusting your loan amount or tenure—to help you qualify safely.
